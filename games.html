08 why me for playtesting

Why I'd be a great playtester

  • Genuine love of games. This isn't a filler job for me — it's my life. I'll care about your titles as much as my own.
  • Sharp attention to detail. For my engineering thesis I ran multi-day test cycles (7–22 days), followed strict procedures and logged every result.
  • Reliable with instructions. Comfortable following structured scenarios, set playstyles and focus points — and reporting clearly.
  • English B2+. Clear written feedback and bug reports. I also speak Polish, Slovak and Russian.
  • On-site in Warsaw. Based in the EU, B.Sc. graduate, ready to work on-site. I've already worked seasonally in Warsaw and Kraków.
